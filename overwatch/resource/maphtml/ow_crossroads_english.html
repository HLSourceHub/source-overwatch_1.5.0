• Backstory

Combine forces have located a top secret rebel bunker built at a crossroads somewhere in Ravenholm. They are tasked to storm in and destroy it, as its destruction would have a lasting crippling effect on the Resistance.

As noise from the conflict has attracted even more zombies to the area surrounding the bunker, the Combine will first have to make their way through the hordes.

The rebels have already called for reinforcements to help fend off the attack. They must now survive against two foes until their fellow Resistance fighters arrive.
• Notes

Crossroads features a common enemy to both sides: Zombies.
